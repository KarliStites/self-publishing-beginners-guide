About the author

Background

Karli Stites works as a Content Designer at Microsoft in Atlanta, Georgia. She graduated from Georgia Tech with a BS Computer Science and has a strong technical background, but she soon transitioned to a more creative position when she moved into technical writing. Currently, Karli is pursuing a MS in Learning Design and Technology with an expected graduation date of December 2022.

Author details

Karli is a tech nerd by day (and also by night), so she did the only thing she could when faced with an overload of creativity that had nowhere to go: she started writing books! She published her first book in May 2019 and she hasn't looked back since. As of July 2022, she's been in six self-published anthologies with other independent writers and has two self-published books of her own. She's very passionate about the indie community and is happy to help new authors get started on their journey!

Fun facts

If Karli isn't reading or writing, you can catch her cuddling with her fur babies, Athena, Major, and Crookshanks, or playing XBOX.
