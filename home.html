Executive summary

The goal of this website is to teach first-time authors how to self-publish an eBook on Amazon. As an independent author myself, I've found that many people I've talked to have expressed interest in publishing a book but they're not sure where to start. Today it's easier than it's ever been to publish something, and everyone has the opportunity to put their work out in the world.

This website is targeted towards first-time authors. I want to help teach you why self-publishing is valuable and how you can take your manuscript and turn it into an eBook to be sold in the Kindle store.

This website includes a brief overview of self-publishing, answers to frequently asked questions about self-publishing, and a detailed breakdown of how to self-publish your book on Amazon.

How it will help you

Hopefully, you'll be able feel confident enough with the steps to publish your manuscript on Amazon! Alternatively, if you don't have a manuscript ready, you should be equipped with the knowledge you need to get started whenever you're done.

What you'll learn

  • The definition of self-publishing
  • The difference between self-publishing and traditional publishing
  • The most common platforms for self-publishing
  • Amazon manuscript compatibility formats
  • How to self-publish your manuscript as an eBook on Amazon

Let's get started!

To learn more about self-publishing or get answers to the most commonly asked questions, go to the FAQs page. You can also walkthrough the detailed process of self-publishing your manuscript on the How to page. If you can't find what you need, you can always contact us for more help.
